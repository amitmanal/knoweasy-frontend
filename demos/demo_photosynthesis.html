Photosynthesis
Question: Explain photosynthesis in simple steps.
Final Answer
Photosynthesis is the process by which green plants make their own food (glucose) using sunlight, water, and carbon dioxide, and release oxygen.
Explanation / Steps
  1. Leaves take in carbon dioxide from air through stomata.
  2. Roots absorb water and send it to leaves.
  3. Chlorophyll traps sunlight energy.
  4. Sunlight energy helps convert water + carbon dioxide into glucose.
  5. Oxygen is released as a by‑product.
Key Insight
Sunlight is the energy source that turns simple raw materials into food.
Optional deeper dive
A common balanced form: 6CO₂ + 6H₂O → C₆H₁₂O₆ + 6O₂ (in presence of sunlight and chlorophyll).

Question: A car starts from rest and accelerates at 2 m/s² for 5 s. Find final velocity and distance.
Final Answer
Final velocity = 10 m/s, Distance = 25 m
Explanation / Steps
  1. Given: u=0, a=2, t=5
  2. Velocity: v = u + at = 0 + 2×5 = 10 m/s
  3. Distance: s = ut + ½at² = 0 + ½×2×25 = 25 m
Key Insight
Pick the formula that matches the given quantities — then substitute cleanly.

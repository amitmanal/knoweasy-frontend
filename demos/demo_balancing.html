Balance the equation
Question: H₂ + O₂ → H₂O
Final Answer
2H₂ + O₂ → 2H₂O
Explanation / Steps
  1. Count atoms on both sides: Left has H=2, O=2; Right has H=2, O=1.
  2. Make oxygen even on the right by putting 2 before H₂O: Right becomes H=4, O=2.
  3. Now hydrogen is 4 on the right, so put 2 before H₂ on the left.
Key Insight
Balancing changes the coefficients, not the formula of compounds.
